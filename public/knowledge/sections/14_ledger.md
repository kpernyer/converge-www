# Ledger

Ledger architecture ensures append-only context, ordering, and auditability.

## Key Points

- Ledger preserves integrity and ordering of facts.
- Merkle trees provide tamper-evident snapshots and proofs.
- Lamport clocks provide causal ordering across contexts.
- Distribution model supports multi-tenant correctness.
- Security policies define access and audit posture.
